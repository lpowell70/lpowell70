Our Methodology

Governance of Artificial intelligence at ESDC is needed in order to establish processes of decision-making among stakeholders involved in developing and implementing AI solutions. The objective of AI governance is to create a system of trust so the department can move forward with developing and procuring valuable AI solutions confidently, knowing that considerations related to privacy, security, ethics, law, transparency, bias and performance are made along the way.

The design of the governance process is formatted as a checklist of questions all AI projects will need to answer in order to provide assurance that the solution has been created responsibly. However, team are not left to figure it all out themselves, the AI support function made up of CDO and IITB employees, will provide development teams advisory support that will help streamline the assurance process and keep team in compliance with the Treasury Board Secretariat Directive on Automated Decision-Making.
