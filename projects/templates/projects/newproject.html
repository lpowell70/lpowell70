Research and Exploration Phase

The objective of this phase is to encourage your team to continue innovating and investigating how AI can be applied to a particular set of data to determine if it can improve a business process or solve a problem.

By completing this section of the assurance tool, you will be on your way to:

  • Ensuring the department has consent to use the data for the purpose(s) of the project
  • Using the available tools and algorithms to support the exploration, such that existing work can be leveraged
  • Confirming the project is aligned with high-level AI ethical principles

 Implementation Phase 

        

          The objective of this phase is not to determine if the solution will be implemented, but rather how will it be delivered and how will we measure its success over time. The business area should work with stakeholders to complete all the assurance initiatives needed to control risks and monitor success over-time. The CDO will provide support on AI specific issues and monitoring support will be provided as needed in conjunction with other areas to track changes to the model over-time and protect against reverse engineering. ITTB will help enable the solutions deployment by setting up infrastructure, accessibility and data management support.
